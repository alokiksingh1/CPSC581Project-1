About Doodle Tales

Welcome to Doodle Tales, where creativity meets cutting-edge technology. My Name is Alokik Singh Kinra. I am computer science student at UofC. Doodle Tales is brought to you by the team group 9 of CPSC 581. Besides me there are three other members in this group : Ariel Lin, Yvonne Ng and Britney Phan
Our platform invites you to unleash your imagination, providing an interactive space to craft unique narratives with the power of AI. Start by offering simple inputs: draw an animal, select vivid adjectives, dynamic verbs, and captivating backgrounds. Within moments, Doodle Tales transforms your choices into a mesmerizing AI-generated story complemented by a one-of-a-kind illustration. But the magic doesn't end there! Each tale and doodle is preserved in a digital album, immortalizing your storytelling adventures. Dive into a fusion of art and technology, and let Doodle Tales redefine your creative horizons.
