Doodle Tales Inspiration

"Doodle Tales" was inspired by the raw emotion and spontaneity of doodles, which often capture fleeting moments of creativity. Our group envisioned a platform where these sketches are amplified with AI-generated narratives, combining visual and textual storytelling. The design emphasizes a seamless interface for users to upload drawings and provide story prompts. Using web technologies like JavaScript and Bootstrap, coupled with an AI backend, the site crafts coherent tales from user inputs, enhancing sketches with AI-generated images. Essentially, "Doodle Tales" marries human artistry with machine intelligence, transforming quick sketches into enriched storytelling moments.

Basic Sketches

The idea behind these basic sketches were drawn from a perspective of a child. As a child, i was always fascinated by these simple games, with very basic features. I started to draw these basic sketches thinking of myself , what would i want in a game or a learning experience. nd from here on, I tried to motivate myself to draw more and more sketches, keeping in mind that I need to mix it up with AI. And also, how can I make it more interactive and fun for the user.

Refine Sketches

Refining the sketches, was not that difficult once all of the group members sat down nd discussed their ideas. One idea led to another, and I was able to come up with these sketches which were based on wht we decided to draw refinements on. Then after drawing and discussing these refinements, we were able to come up with a final sketch, which we thought would be the best to implement. This final sketch had its own story, because we were back to stage 1 of sketching. The Doodle Tales sketch is the final implementation for our project as well.

Final Implementation

To implement Doodle Tales, the sketch had to come to life. We started by searching all the APIs that we could use to implement our idea. And then after a lot of trial and error, a lot of CSS and JS implementations , this is how our final project looks like. This is an interactive design game/ learning experience for kids, where they can draw their own sketches and then the AI will generate a story for them. The users are also prompted to choose the adjective and the verb they would like to relate their animal with, and then after finally choosing the setting of the story, the AI will generate the image and the story accordingly. The user can also choose to draw again, if they are not satisfied with the story or the image. And also, whatever they draw is stored in their album, which they can access anytime they want.
